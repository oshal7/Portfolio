00 / overview

An accessibility mandate inside Axis Design Labs

During my master's thesis with the DBAT (Digital Banking & Technology) design team at Axis Design Labs, I collaborated directly with Axis Bank. My primary focus was enhancing digital accessibility across the bank's products, specifically for users with disabilities — while also contributing to several other initiatives within the Labs.

01 / the environment

Working across a multi-pod design ecosystem

Axis Design Labs is the design team behind Axis Bank's digital platforms. It's organised into multiple pods — Research, Communication, Motion Design, Interaction, and Design System — each shaping and innovating the bank's digital offerings. Being embedded here meant working inside a dynamic, cross-disciplinary environment rather than a single siloed team.

02 / scope of work

Elevating accessibility through a strategic roadmap

My core work centred on raising the digital accessibility of Axis Bank's products for people with disabilities. This meant developing a strategic roadmap — in close collaboration with business units, the design team, and external stakeholders. I operated at the intersection of two pods:

Design System team

Embedding accessibility into shared components and patterns, so it scaled by default rather than being bolted on per product.

Research team

Grounding the roadmap in real user needs — audits, desk research, and conversations with subject-matter experts.

03 / roles & responsibilities

What I owned as sole contributor

As the single contributor on the accessibility initiative, my responsibilities spanned research, strategy, and stakeholder work — the combination that made the roadmap possible.

01

Accessibility audits

Systematically evaluating existing products against accessibility standards to establish a baseline and surface priority gaps.

02

Desk research & SME discussions

Studying best practices and facilitating conversations with subject-matter experts to shape a credible, informed approach.

03

Solution generation

Turning audit findings into actionable, innovative solutions that fit within Axis Bank's design system and constraints.

04

Strategy & stakeholder cooperation

Implementing strategies and keeping business, design, and external stakeholders aligned toward a shared accessibility roadmap.

04 / other contributions

Beyond the primary accessibility project

In the Design System team

Slider component for all Axis Bank products

Led the design of slider components to improve consistency and user experience across the bank's digital products, feeding a more seamless design system.

Patterns for the Subzero design system

Created design patterns for critical elements — logins, forms, OTPs, loaders, assisted flows, and MPIN — improving cohesiveness across offerings.

In the Research team

User interviews for Axis AMC users

Conducted interview research for Axis AMC (Asset Management Company) users alongside senior researchers — designing research plans, running interviews, and synthesising feedback.

Guerrilla testing for fund transfer

Collaborated on guerrilla testing for the fund-transfer module — planning research, crafting questions, designing tasks, and executing them in the field.
